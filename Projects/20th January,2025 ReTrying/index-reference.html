Most prompters force you to pick one single speed for your presentation — as if you were a monotonous robot.

FluidPrompter lets you slooow down, SPEED UP and ... pause your prompter. So you can look and sound like your natural human self (minus the fumbling).

HARDWARE BRAND AGNOSTIC

When it comes to controllers, you’re in control

Most prompters lock you into using their own white-label controller. Which is a shame, because controls are such a personal thing.

FluidPrompter is hardware agnostic and works with a wide variety of controllers. Including keyboard, mouse, remotes, switches, gamepad controllers, stream decks and pedals.
